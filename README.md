## 🔍 Key Insights

* Movies dominate Netflix content compared to TV shows
* Significant growth in content after 2015
* Certain genres like Drama and Comedy are most popular
* USA contributes the highest number of titles
* Majority of content falls under specific rating categories

---

## 💡 Recommendations

* Focus more on high-demand genres like Drama & Comedy
* Increase regional content production for diversity
* Invest in newer content trends based on growth patterns
* Optimize content duration based on audience preference
